## June 27 Relationship Decision Frame — Mutual Movement, Not Longing

When Steve is preparing for the June 27 reconnection with Teresa, keep the deciding question clean:

**Not:** "Do we love each other?" / "Did we miss each other?" / "Is there warmth, chemistry, longing, or hope?"

**Yes:** "Is there concrete mutual movement, or just longing dressed up as hope?"

The adult-relationship frame is:

> Does Teresa have enough to offer an adult relationship with Steve, given that Steve now understands his wound and is working with it?

This means Steve stops asking Teresa to heal the boy, but Teresa also cannot make adult Steve live in the dark. The shared bridge is:

- Steve manages the abandonment wound.
- Teresa manages the impact of her withdrawal.

### Concrete movement from Teresa would look like

- Real therapy, or at minimum a concrete appointment, plus understanding why outside help matters.
- Naming her part without Steve dragging it out of her.
- Speaking specifically about what happens inside her when she shuts down.
- Offering a realistic tether during space: "I'm overwhelmed, not leaving, I need tonight, I'll check in tomorrow."
- Saying what she wants, not only "if you want to."
- Understanding that surface-level safety for her can still be lonely for Steve.
- Participating in repair after shutdown instead of only moving on once the emotional weather changes.

### Concrete movement from Steve would look like

- Stating needs without prosecuting Teresa.
- Hearing "I need space" without making it mean abandonment.
- Not using relationship conversations to regulate a spike.
- Tolerating short-term uncertainty without forcing certainty.
- Wanting depth as a legitimate intimacy need, without demanding immediate depth to feel safe.

### The decision rule

If June 27 is mostly love, ache, chemistry, warmth, missing each other, and hope, **do not restart the relationship.** That is the old cycle.

If June 27 shows concrete movement on both sides, the answer is still not "go back to normal." The only viable yes is a structured, time-bound restart with therapy, space rules, repair rules, and no pretending ambiguity is connection.

Session detail lives in `references/june-27-mutual-movement-frame.md`.
